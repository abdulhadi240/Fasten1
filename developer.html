How Fasten Connect Works

Fasten Connect enables fast, secure, and HIPAA-compliant data sharing. Developers can integrate patient data access quickly, streamlining workflows across 30,000+ healthcare systems.

For Developers

Fasten Connect provides the tools you need to connect your applications to a vast network of healthcare data sources. With just a few lines of code, you can integrate patient data into your application, securely and in real-time.
Sign Up for Fasten Connect
Create an account to access our tools and resources.
Get Your API Keys
Receive the credentials needed to connect to our network of healthcare systems.
Easily Integrate
Integrate Fasten Connect API to share patient data securely in real-time.

For Patients

Fasten Connect helps patients take control of their healthcare data by giving them secure and instant access to their medical records.
Discover Your Health Network
Find your doctor, hospital, or health plan in the Fasten Connect network.
Authenticate and Permit Access
Log in using your current provider or health plan details, then give permission to share your data securely.
Access Your Medical Data
Fasten Connect organizes health records and insurance claims in real-time.

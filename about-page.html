About Us

We started Fasten Health to make healthcare data more accessible, secure, and easy to manage for everyone. Because your health should never be stuck in outdated systems.

Meet Jason Kulatunga

CEO
Jason Kulatunga

In 2024, Jason, the founder of Fasten Health, was frustrated by how difficult it was to access his own medical records.

 

From paper forms to faxes and disconnected systems, he found himself wasting valuable time just trying to get the information he needed. That experience led him to realize: This problem wasn’t unique, it was affecting patients, providers, payers, and developers alike.

 

That's when Jason decided to create Fasten Health, a platform that could break down the barriers to accessing and sharing healthcare data.

Our Mission
Fasten Health's mission is simple: to empower people to take control of their healthcare data while enabling healthcare organizations to work smarter and more efficiently.
Our Vision
We aim to make medical records instantly accessible, securely shared, and fully protected by the highest privacy standards.

The Data Problem & How Fasten Connect Fixes It

Right now, accessing your medical records feels like a challenge.

 

Data is fragmented, time is wasted, and critical care decisions are delayed. This inefficiency costs both time and money, and it puts patients at risk.

 

Fasten Connect solves this problem by linking over 30,000 health systems with one unified API, bringing real-time data to your fingertips. Secure, simple, and efficient—Fasten Connect makes healthcare work better for everyone.
